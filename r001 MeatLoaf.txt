Meat Loaf

2 lbs (908 g) ground meat
1.5 cup bread crumbs
0.75 cup milk
1 package dry onion soup mix
14 oz (340 g) can mushrooms
2 eggs
0.25 cup ketchup
0.25 tablespoon salt
0.25 tablespoon pepper

Chop mushrooms save juice add it to the milk
and soak dry or state bread crumbs in it to make 
1.5 cups. Mix all ingredients. Bake 1.75 hours at
300F (150C)
