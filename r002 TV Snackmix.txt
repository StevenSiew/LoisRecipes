TV Snackmix

1 package Rice Chex
1 package Cheerios
1 package wheat chex
1 package pretzels
2 lbs (908 g) mixed nuts

mix together 1 lbs (454 g) butter
2 tablespoon celery salt
2 tablespoon onion salt
2 tablespoon garlic salt
1.5 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoon worsisture sauce

heat and pour over cereal
bake 2 hours at 250F (93 C)
stir every 15 minutes
