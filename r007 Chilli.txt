Chilli

1.5 lbs (680 g) hamburger 
2 large onion chopped
1 cup green pepper chopped
2 cloves garlic minced
2 14.5 oz (411 g) canned tomatoes cutter up
2 15 oz (435 g) cans kidney beans drained
2 8 oz (226 g) can tomato sauce (Tomato Paste Australia)
4-6 teaspoons chilli powder
1 teaspoon chilli powder
1 teaspoon dried basil crushed
0.5 teaspoon pepper

Cook meat with onion pepper and garlic.
Drain and combined with other ingredients in crockpot.
Cook on low for 8-10 hours.
Or on high for 5 hours.
