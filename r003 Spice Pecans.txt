Spiced Pecans

1 lbs (454 g) Pecans Halves
1 egg white
1 teaspoon cold water
a dash of salt
0.5 cup sugar
0.5 teaspoon of cinnamon

Beat egg white and water until frothy
Add pecans, stir until wet
Mix salt sugar and cinnamon
Add to pecan and blend until pecan are coated
Use cookie sheet with sides
Bake for 1 hour 250F (120 C) stir every 15 minutes
