Fondue Italiano

1 lbs (454 g) lean ground beef
1 envelope spaghetti source mix
2 15 oz (2 x 425 g) can tomato sauce (tomato paste)
1 lbs (454 g) sharp cheddar cheese
8 oz (226 g) Mozzarella cheese shredded 
2 tablespoons corn starch
0.5 cup dry red wine

In a skillet cook beef until crumbly
combine beef and sauce mix cheeses
covered and cook for two hours low
dissolve corn starch in red wine
turn control to high
add dissolved corn starch
heat on high for 10-15 minutes
dip chunks of Italian bread
