Beef Log

5 lbs (908 g) lean hamburger
2.5 teaspoons coarse pepper
5 rounded top cartons tender quick salt
2.5 teaspoons whole mustard seeds
2.5 teaspoons garlic salt
2 teaspoons celery seeds
1 teaspoon dill seeds
2.5 teaspoons liquid smoke

Mix in bowl. Cover and refrigerate.
Remove once a day for three days.
And knead well for 5 minutes.
On fourth day divide into 6 sausage like rolls.
Lay rolls on drip pan or boiler pan.
Bake 9 hours at 160F (71 C)
Turn at 4.5 hours.
At the end of 4 hours, drain grease.
Refrigerate or freeze.
